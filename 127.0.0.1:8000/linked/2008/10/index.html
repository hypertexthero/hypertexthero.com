Water Law and Standards  #

Water Law and Standards

Website HTML and Stylesheet design

Problem

IT Works developed a website for Water Law and Standards, a joint project of the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) and the World Health Organization (WHO).

The simplest HTML and CSS was required in order to maintain maximum accessibility for users of the site in countries where internet connections are very slow and computers are very old.

Solution

Hypertexthero was called in and created accessible HTML templates and a simple stylesheet.

Work Done

HTML markup, CSS. Visit site →

City Index Australia  #

City Index Australia

Website design

Problem

City Index Australia needed a way to clearly communicate the simplicty of their contracts for difference (CFD) services to an audience accustomed to seeing a great deal of financial information on a daily basis.

Solution

Simon worked together with Karim Gargum to create a clear, easily navigable website built on a foundation of web standards. The simplicity of the site quickly communicates City Index Australia’s services.

Work Done

Graphic design, XHTML markup, CSS, CMS templates and implementation. Note: Hypertexthero is no longer in control of the City Index Australia website design.

Recommended Restaurants in Rome  # 03 feb 2012

1) Da Jenny – http://www.2spaghi.it/ristoranti/lazio/rm/roma/osteria-jenny-a-san-lorenzo/ – in a more ‘ghetto’ area, with true Romans instead of toursits. Closed Tuesday.

2) Da Teo (used to be called Da Enzo) – http://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurant_Review-g187791-d850382-Reviews-Trattoria_da_Teo-Rome_Lazio.html – probably my favorite restaurant in Rome. In Trastevere. Has tourists but the place is local. Closed Sunday.

3) Da Baffetto (only open at night I believe) – http://maps.google.com/maps/place?q=rome+da+baffetto+pizza&cid=12153075406193498165 a good pizza amid lots of Italian confusion.

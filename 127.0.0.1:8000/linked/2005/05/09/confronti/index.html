Confronti  # 09 may 2005

Confronti

Website redesign

Problem

Confronti is an Italian magazine which promotes dialogue between religions. The small staff have their hands full producing the printed magazine and found it difficult to keep their website content synchronized with print editions. The exisiting site archive was difficult to navigate and the website’s look and feel was inconsistent with the Confronti brand.

Solution

HypertextHero redesigned the site, bringing it up-to-date with the printed brand and with modern standards-based design techniques including a clean typographical hierarchy powered by CSS. This made both navigation and reading of the extensive articles an easier, more pleasant experience for users. Textpattern, a simple yet powerful publishing system was installed allowing the busy staff to update the website quickly and without expert HTML knowledge. Powered by open-source plugins from the Textpattern community, the site archive became automated freeing yet more time for Confronti’s important work.

Free OS X Apps I Use  # 10 jul 2006

- Adium X, multi-protocol chat: http://adiumx.com/ – Quicksilver, interface for working with applications, contacts, music, and other data: http://quicksilver.blacktree.com/ – Font Explorer X, great font manager: http://www.linotype.com/fontexplorerX – VLC, media/video player supporting multiple formats: http://www.videolan.org/vlc/ – NeoOffice, word, excel, powerpoint, open source: http://www.neooffice.org/ – Textwrangler, text editor for making websites, programming, etc: http://www.barebones.com/products/textwrangler/ – Cyberduck, FTP client to upload/download files to a server: http://cyberduck.ch/ – Skype, voice over internet protocol client: http://skype.com – Poisoned, file-sharing P2P client: http://gottsilla.net/ – Vienna, RSS/Atom newsreader: http://www.opencommunity.co.uk/vienna2.php – Colloquy, internet relay chat client: http://colloquy.info/ – Google Earth, http://earth.google.com/ – Carbon Copy Cloner, backup, cloning data: http://www.bombich.com/software/ccc.html – Pixen, Pixel graphics editor: http://www.opensword.org/Pixen/

AOSR 2008–2009 Calendar  # 06 feb 2009

h1. American Overseas School of Rome

Calendar design

h2. Problem

AOSR needs a calendar that is useful for its staff and also provides a promotional opportunity for the school to send to parents of potential students.

h2. Solution

Hypertexthero photographed student artwork, designed an eye-catching, highly legible calendar and oversaw its production.

h2. Work Done

Artwork photography, graphic design, layout and typesetting, printing consultation.

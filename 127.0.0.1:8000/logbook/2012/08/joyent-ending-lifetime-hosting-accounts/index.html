Joyent Ending "Lifetime" Hosting Accounts

Friday, August 17 2012

Update, 31 August 2012: And TextDrive is back, the service will continue. Thank you, Dean and Jason.

Email sent to Joyent today:

I purchased a Textdrive/Joyent VC lifetime account for 399 USD in 2005. It was a lot of money for me at the time and I trusted Dean Allen’s and Jason Hoffman’s promise.

I’m disappointed in your decision to break that promise to the people who funded you in the beginning, especially given that Joyent is a successful company now.

I would like:

1) A full refund of the 399 USD I paid (I can provide my bank details in case you don’t have them on record anymore),
2) The deadline for moving away from your service extended to the end of the year (I am not interested in being your customer any longer with your cloud offering, seeing how you have changed from your initial vision of Textdrive/Joyent as a company who cared about independent web publishing).

Also, a public explanation and apology from your CEO would help limit the damage done to your reputation. I recommended you to a lot of clients over the years. I am disappointed.

Simon Griffee

What the Hell Are You Using to Give Presentations?

Monday, November 28 2011

PowerPoint? Keynote? SlideShare?

Please, I beg you, stop doing this. Stop this madness.

Instead, consider using a chalkboard, or a simple HTML and CSS page (demo).1

Using simple tools to aid you in presenting your ideas will make your message clearer to both you and your audience, making you both happier and more engaged with each other.

  1. Two other options are Remark (demo)and S5 (demo). ↩

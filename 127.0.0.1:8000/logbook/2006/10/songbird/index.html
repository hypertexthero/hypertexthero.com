Songbird  # 25 oct 2006
I’m getting tired of Apple’s continuous release of iTunes ‘updates’ that build walls around my music, making it more difficult to move it between computers and iPods and breaking functionality in the name of damned DRM. With many young, intelligent people working on things like Songbird, iTunes will eventually go the way Internet Explorer is going when faced with Firefox.

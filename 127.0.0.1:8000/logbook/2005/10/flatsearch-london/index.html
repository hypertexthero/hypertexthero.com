FlatSearch London

Tuesday, October 11 2005

FlatSearchLondon.com

One-page website design

Problem

Peggy O’Mahony has been renting flats in central London long before the web existed. She doesn’t have a computer and wanted a simple webpage to point customers to and aid them in recommending her business to others coming to London in need of a good place to stay.

Solution

As one of Peggy’s happy customers himself, Hypertexthero created a simple, attractive one-page website with valid HTML and CSS and plenty of room to grow.

Work Done

Graphic design, HTML markup and CSS.

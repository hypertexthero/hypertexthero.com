Who Knew  # 05 sep 2005

Who Knew provides resources including the decoding of “cryptic and deceptive language used by credit card companies (The first step in the process of taking control of your credit.)”:http://www.whoknew.org/content/q10/content.html, international graphic design graduate “requirements for legal status in the US (Economic, cultural and legal issues that US International graphic design graduates are confronted with upon graduation, including alumni testimonials and the relevant options and processes for obtaining legal status in the US.)”:http://www.whoknew.org/content/q13/content.html and an exploration into the “facts and misconceptions about bottled water (H20 Hype.)”:http://www.whoknew.org/content/q05/content.html. Unfortunately the site uses HTML(HyperText Markup Language) frames, which everyone knows are “bowls of porridge that belong to bears (HTML dog.)”:http://www.htmldog.com/guides/htmlintermediate/badtags/.

Entrepreneur 27  # 29 nov 2005

h3. Entrepreneur 27

Website design

h4. Problem

Entrepreneur 27 needed a website to promote its future events to students and young professionals. The site would also serve as a primary location to refer venture capitalists and other influential people about E27’s conferences and its brand. The site needed to be up pronto.

h4. Solution

Hyperrexthero sprung into action with a layout mock-up that was quickly approved. Minimal graphics were used in an elegant combination of markup and style sheets in accordance with “web standards (Find out why web standards are important at the Web Standards Project website.)”:http://www.webstandards.org/about/. The site was built in less than a week using the agility of “Textpattern (Free, flexible, elegant, easy-to-use content management system.)”:http://textpattern.com.

h4. Work Done

Graphic design, XHTML(Extensible Hypertext Markup Language) markup, CSS(Cascading Style Sheets), template development and implementation. Note: Hypertexthero is no longer in control of the “Entrepreneur 27”:http://entrepreneur27.org website design.

KJAZ

Thursday, October 28 2010

A regular of Hypertexthero’s working-hours soundtrack is The Spirit of KJAZ radio station, most often hosted by the most excellent, late Jerry Dean. KJAZ is limited to the internet medium these days (iTunes → Radio → Jazz → The Spirit of Jazz), a consequence of government and corporate greed which should be fought.

Update 28 October 2010: It looks like KJAZ is not listed in iTunes anymore! Listen through Shoutcast here (you can drag this link to your iTunes window to save a bookmark and listen from there) or using the embedded player below. Also check out KCSM’s Jazz 91.

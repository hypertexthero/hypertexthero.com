{% endcomment %} {# #}

I work with photography, art direction, visual design and editing to feel and observe and then craft and convey information that hopefully helps make our world a better place. I have diverse technical skills, enjoy learning new things and fancy myself an amateur Treasure Hunter.

Born and raised in Brazil, my childhood was spent on the move there. I learned English in Tunisia and to see in Italy. I currently live in New York City where I work part of my time as a designer and printer in Milton Glaser’s studio and at Columbia University.

Project Brief

Your name:
Your name and title here.

Your organization name and location:
Organization name and location here.

Your email address:
you@domain.tld

Your business phone:
Please include area and country code.

Describe your organization:
Briefly, who are you, what do you do, why does it matter? If you have an existing website, what is its URL?

Describe the concept, product or service you intend to promote and the medium you have in mind for the project (website, brochure, print ads, etc):
Your answer.

Describe your audience:
Who are they? What do they do? What do they think and feel?

Describe the primary purpose of your project:
What primary goal do you wish to accomplish by promoting your concept, product or service to your audience? What do you want your audience to think and feel when they hear your voice? What does this project need to accomplish at the very least?

Describe your brand’s tone of voice:
In as few words or sentences as possible, describe the feel you wish your concept, product or service to evoke in your audience. Warm? Serious? Playful? Hi-tech? Humorous? Classic? Modern? Understated? Punk rock?

Additional notes and comments:
Please use as much detail as you need.
